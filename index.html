Features

Everything you need
to train your way

From quick interval timers to detailed daily journals, SetGo adapts to how you work out.

⏱

Interval Timer

Fully customizable work, rest, and HIIT timers. Set your rounds, adjust durations, and let SetGo handle the transitions.

📝

Workout Journal

Set daily goals for each day of the week. Track reps, time, and completion. See your progress build day by day.

💪

Custom Exercises

Create your own exercises, save workout presets, and access them instantly. Your library, your way.

⚡

HIIT & Tabata

Built-in support for HIIT and Tabata workouts. Configure intervals, rest periods, and rounds to match your training style.

🎨

Themes & Dark Mode

Choose from multiple color themes. Switch between light and dark mode. Make SetGo feel like yours.

📲

Home Screen Widget

See your daily goals at a glance without opening the app. Track progress right from your home screen.
