Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

1 Information We Collect

SetGo collects minimal data to provide you with the best experience. This includes:

  • Workout history and exercise configurations stored locally on your device
  • Subscription status managed securely through RevenueCat
  • Anonymous usage analytics to improve app performance

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or location unless you voluntarily provide it through our support form.

2 Data Storage

Your workout data, journal entries, presets, and preferences are stored locally on your device using secure storage. We do not upload your workout data to external servers.

Subscription information is processed and stored by RevenueCat and the respective app store (Apple App Store or Google Play Store) under their own privacy policies.

3 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat - Subscription management and payment processing
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store - App distribution and in-app purchases
  • Expo - App framework and push notification delivery

Each service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policies.

4 Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. All subscription transactions are processed securely through the App Store or Google Play Store. Your local data is protected by your device's built-in security features.

5 Children's Privacy

SetGo is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children unless they have been granted permission by a parent or legal guardian. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data without parental consent, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected within the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7 Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at ysoftwareapp@gmail.com or through the Contact Support option in Settings.
